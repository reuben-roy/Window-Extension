1 / 12 Window starts where attention is won or lost: inside the browser.
The problem

Dreams rarely die in one moment. They erode through tiny context switches.

The founder tracked his own work patterns for 90 days and found the same pattern most ambitious people feel but cannot measure: attention fragments faster than progress compounds.

Browser work is interruption-heavy

Work, communication, research, and distraction live in the same interface.

Existing tools split the workflow

Blockers stop sites. Note apps store ideas. Planners rearrange calendars. None protect the whole loop.

Good ideas become bad interruptions

Users either chase the idea and lose focus, or ignore it and lose the idea.

Illustrative reconstruction from the founder's 90-day self-tracking

The point is not exact percentages. The point is how often attention splinters across parallel threads before real work gets finished.

2 / 12 Founder insight: the real enemy is fragmented attention, not lack of ambition.
Why now

Three shifts make this category newly winnable.

01

Work collapsed into the browser

Knowledge work now happens across docs, GitHub, chat, research, and admin in a single tabbed environment.

02

AI can process ideas asynchronously

Users no longer need to break focus to evaluate every thought immediately. Good ideas can wait without being lost.

03

Mainstream users will not self-host complexity

Automation stacks and local agents are powerful, but setup friction still blocks broad adoption. The browser is a lower-friction wedge.

The gap in the market is between blockers that only say "no" and AI work suites that never touch the moment of distraction.
Window is designed to own that moment.

Business model

Price Window above commodity blockers and below full AI work suites.

Free

$0 / month

Calendar connection, basic blocking, limited rules, standard breaks, and enough value to prove the product quickly.

Pro

$12 / month

$96 annual for serious solo users who want unlimited rules, keyword matching, points and streaks, idea capture, and limited monthly AI reports.

Team

$15 / user / month

Annual plan after team features mature: shared policies, pooled AI usage, admin reporting, onboarding, and billing controls.

Pricing logic

  • Blockers alone are cheap and hard to differentiate.
  • Window earns higher willingness to pay by combining focus control with useful assistant behavior.
  • AI usage is limited in Pro to protect margins while still demonstrating premium value.
  • Team pricing stays in line with productivity SaaS once admin controls exist.
